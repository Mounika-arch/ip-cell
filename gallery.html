2023 Events

On February 10, 2023, a guest lecture on "IP Filing and Commercialization" was held at Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College, organized in collaboration with MHRD-IIC and the CSI-Student’s Branch.

Ms. Elizabeth Puthran, a renowned Patent and Trademark Attorney from Puthran & Associates, delivered an insightful talk on the significance of intellectual property rights for faculty members.

Dr. M.S. Geetha Devasena, Professor of CSE, provided the opening remarks, welcoming attendees and setting the context for the importance of the topic discussed.

Ms. Elizabeth Puthran, the guest lecturer, receives an award from Grace Selvarani, Head of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, recognizing her valuable contribution to the seminar on IP Filing and Commercialization.

On July 22, 2023, the SREC IP Cell hosted a session on patent drafting led by Patent Attorney Mr. Muruganantham, attended by 70 faculty members. The session covered the process of patenting ideas, including writing descriptions and claims, with opening remarks by Dr. M. S. Geetha Devasena.

2022 Events

The Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College conducted an Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) awareness session on March 25, 2022, under the National Intellectual Property Awareness Mission (NIPAM). Mr. P. Hariprasath, Examiner of Patents & Designs from the Intellectual Property Office, Chennai, delivered a detailed talk on IPR.

The session introduced over 1500 students and 72 faculty members to various forms of intellectual property rights, fostering creativity and encouraging them to innovate. The program ignited intellectual curiosity, guiding participants on how to turn their innovative ideas into valuable IP assets.

Under the Government of India's NIPAM initiative, the awareness program on IPR was a major success at SREC, providing students and faculty with knowledge on patents, copyrights, and more. The event marked a significant step in nurturing future innovators.
