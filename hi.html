Intellectual Property Rights

Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College encourages, facilitates, promotes, and safeguards scientific investigations and research carried out. The IPR policy of Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College provides guidelines for making patents to faculty members, students, and those who work in industries. The IP policy of Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College aims to lay down the process for promotion and support to inventors for translating their creative works into IP and also assists in the attempts to sell/license the technology.

Know Your Rights

Intellectual property (IP) refers to various distinct types of creations of the mind: inventions, literary and artistic works, and symbols, names, images, and designs used in commerce; for which a set of exclusive rights like copyrights, trademarks, patents, industrial design rights, and trade secrets are recognized under the corresponding fields of law.
