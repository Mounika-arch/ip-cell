NIPAM

NIPAM (National Intellectual Property Awareness Mission) is an initiative aimed at fostering awareness of intellectual property rights across academic institutions. The program helps students and faculty understand the importance of protecting intellectual innovations and provides a platform for enhancing their knowledge about patents, copyrights, and trademarks.

Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College proudly recognizes the active participation in the National Intellectual Property Awareness Mission (NIPAM), launched by the Government of India during the 75th Independence anniversary under the "Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav" initiative. The college's exceptional contribution in organizing an awareness program on March 25, 2022, in association with the Intellectual Property Office, Chennai, is highly appreciated.

Dr. Geetha Devasena has been awarded a certificate for her active participation in the National Intellectual Property Awareness Mission (NIPAM). Her significant contributions to promoting awareness of intellectual property rights have been commendable.
